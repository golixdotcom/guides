 How to Deposit Kenyan Shilling (KES) on Golix


Assuming you have already logged in to your account, kindly follow these simple steps:-

    • Click on your KES Wallet as indicated in the diagram below
      
	






    • Click  on the Deposit Tab and fill in  how much  you want deposit and click on CHECKOUT WITH DUSUPAY,  as shown in the diagram below.



    • Select your payment  option  from the list that you wish to use e,g Mpesa Mobile Money or Kenya Local Cards.
    • Fill in your details  eg Phone number, name , Email (optional).
    • Each time you make a deposit make sure  it appears on you Transaction History.
